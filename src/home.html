WORKSHOP

ABOUT

The 7th Japan-Taiwan Workshop on Crustal Dynamics (JTWCD7) will be held from February 11 to 13, 2025, at the National Central University in Taoyuan, Taiwan. The workshop aims to provide a forum where geoscientists can exchange their research findings, share experiences, and establish future collaborations between Taiwan and Japan across all fields of crustal dynamics. Topics such as earthquake hazard mitigation and advancements in remote sensing applications are also welcome.

Location

Teaching & Research Building, National Central University, No. 300, Zhongda Rd., Zhongli Dist., Taoyuan City
